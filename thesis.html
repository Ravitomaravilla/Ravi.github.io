My Thesis

Explore my research work and supporting materials here:

My thesis explores the velocity dispersion profiles of early-type galaxies using integral field spectroscopic data from the MaNGA (Mapping Nearby Galaxies at Apache Point Observatory) survey. I am studying how stellar kinematics and velocity dispersion reveal insights into the formation histories and internal structures of galaxies. This work combines observational data analysis . This work applies kinemetry to map the detailed kinematic structures of early-type galaxies, allowing me to get velocity dispersion, and higher-order kinematic moments at different regions of galaxies. This approach provides a direct link between observed stellar motions and the underlying dynamical processes, helping to unravel how mass distribution, mergers, and evolutionary history shape the internal structure of galaxies.
